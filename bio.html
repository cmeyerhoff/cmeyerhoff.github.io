Joe Schmoe's Biography

Joe was born in Washington DC in 1994. He grew up with a very active lifestyle that continues to this day: he loves soccer, rock climbing, running, and hiking. Joe attended Sidwell Friends School before continuing on to Tufts University where he will complete his studies in Engineering Science in the Fall of 2017.

Joe's primary interests are in renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, finance, and the intersections between each of those topics. He hopes to find and work on novel applications of technology to the above fields.
